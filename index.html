Boxing Exhibitions

Starting in December, Bulldog Boxing will be hosting friendly open exhibitons every Saturday from 11am to close. This event is open to all fighters of every age and gender wanting to test their skills. Fighters will be matched up by weight and experience to ensure fair matches. These events will be closly monitered and supervised by the training staff to ensure safety conditions for all fighters.
